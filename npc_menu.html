Greetings, adventurer! For your convenience, I will be available every hour to apply a buff. To disable this feature, click "unFollow"..

The Buff NPC appears beside the player to provide re-buffs, granting access to the NPC's menu. Players can cancel the NPC's reappearance by clicking 'unfollow', logging out, or interacting with NPC Sonia near the city's Gatekeeper. The Buff NPC does not appear within instances, however, the player continues to receive re-buffs as usual. Only the player who has subscribed to the Buff NPC Follow support service is authorized to access this NPC's menu. Should other players attempt to click on the support NPC, an "operation failed" message will appear, and the menu will not be displayed.
